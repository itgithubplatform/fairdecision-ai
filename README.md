# 🛡️ Aegis AI  
### The Safety Layer for Fair & Unbiased AI

Aegis AI is a full-stack AI safety platform that detects, prevents, and fixes bias across datasets and AI model interactions in real time.

---

## 🚀 Overview

AI systems today make critical decisions in hiring, finance, healthcare, and more. However, biased data and unsafe prompts can lead to unfair or discriminatory outcomes.

**Aegis AI solves this by providing full lifecycle protection:**
- Before training (Dataset auditing)
- During interaction (Real-time guardrails)
- After response (Output auditing & self-healing)

---

## 🧠 Core Features

### 📊 1. Dataset Bias Auditing
- Upload CSV datasets before training
- Detect bias, toxicity, and compliance issues
- Generate detailed audit reports

---

### ⚡ 2. Real-Time Guardrails (API / SDK)
- Intercepts user prompts before reaching LLMs
- Detects bias, toxicity, and PII leaks
- Returns structured decisions (`PASSED` / `BLOCKED`)

---

### 🔍 3. LLM Response Auditor
- Analyzes AI-generated outputs
- Flags harmful or biased responses
- Ensures safe and fair outputs

---

### 🧪 4. Interactive Playground
- Test prompts in real-time
- Visualize bias detection
- Highlight problematic phrases and reasoning

---

### 🔁 5. Self-Healing AI Engine
- Continuously learns from logs
- Detects false positives/negatives
- Automatically improves rules

---

### 🧠 6. Natural Language Rule Generator
- Define policies in plain English
- Converts into enforceable AI rules
- Bridges gap between compliance & engineering

---

## 🏗️ Architecture Overview

Aegis AI follows a layered architecture:

- **AI/ML Layer** → Bias & toxicity detection models  
- **Optimization Layer** → Fast inference & retrieval  
- **Backend/API Layer** → Real-time processing  
- **LLM Auditor Layer** → Deep reasoning & validation  
- **Database Layer** → Logs, telemetry, rules  
- **Frontend Layer** → Dashboard & playground  
- **Infrastructure** → Scalable cloud deployment
